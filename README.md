# 🌾 KrushiSetu – Direct Farmer to Buyer Marketplace

KrushiSetu is a localized agricultural marketplace platform designed to connect **farmers and NGOs (sellers)** directly with **buyers such as hotels, manufacturers, and individuals**.

The goal is to eliminate middlemen, improve farmer profits, and create transparent, trust-based trading within regions like Rajkot and Jamnagar, Gujarat.

---

## 🚀 Project Vision

In many regions, farmers depend on intermediaries to sell crops, often receiving unfair prices.  
KrushiSetu enables:

- Direct negotiation between buyers and sellers  
- Flexible online or offline transactions  
- Localized trade within a defined region  
- Trust-building through reviews and verification  

This project is currently under active development and will evolve feature-by-feature.

---

## 🧑‍🌾 User Roles

### 1️⃣ Sellers (Farmers / NGOs)
- Create product listings (crop type, quantity, price, location)
- Upload product images
- Receive offers from buyers
- Accept, reject, or counter offers
- Manage profile and verification status

### 2️⃣ Buyers (Hotels / Manufacturers / Individuals)
- Post product requirements
- Browse seller listings
- Send offers
- Negotiate pricing and quantity
- Leave reviews after completed trades

### 3️⃣ Admin
- Monitor listings and offers
- Manage users
- Approve or suspend accounts
- View analytics (future phase)
